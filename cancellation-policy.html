reWAXation Cancellation Policy

Please arrive 10-15 minutes early for your appointment, we are typically booked back-to-back and cannot guarantee that we will be able to take late arrivals. Clients that come too late to be seen for their appointments will be marked as late cancels/no-shows.

Like most spas, our estheticians get paid a straight commission, so when clients no-show/late cancel they make $0.

Late cancels/late reschedules/no-shows (within 24 hours of appointment) will be charged 50% of their service price which goes directly to their esthetician.
