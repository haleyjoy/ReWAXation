Welcome

At reWAXation, we don't do nails, we don't do facials, we don't do massages... We wax and sugar you smooth. And we do it good.
We don't beat around the bush; we remove it.

We specialize in Brazilian Waxing but offer a full array of hair removal services. From brows and lips to legs and arms and everything in between; both men and women.

Part of what makes our Brazilians, Bikinis, and Betweenies less painful than other salons is our hard wax. With a low melting point, the wax will never burn you. And when applied to a layer of oil, the wax will stick only to your hair and not your skin resulting in a less irritating wax. We use soft strip wax for legs, arms, face, chest, neck, back, shoulders, and other areas as well.

Most of our estheticians are trained in Sugaring. Sugaring is a similar process to waxing, except it's performed by applying a paste made from a mixture of sugar, lemon, and water. When applied to the skin, the sugar paste is close to room temp, again, never burning the skin. The hair is then removed in the same direction as hair growth resulting in a more gentle experience. On top of waxing and sugaring, we offer spray tanning for those occasions when showing up like a pasty white ghost simply is not an option. The tanning solution we use is organic, vegan, and made from naturally derived ingredients. It comes in a variety of tones to get you the perfect color.

Our online booking system allows clients to schedule and manage appointments at their convenience. Our repeat pricing makes our high-quality services some of the most affordable around. Check out the reviews on Yelp!, Google, and Facebook.
